Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026
Your privacy matters. This policy explains what data QuoteBoss collects, how we use it, who we share it with, and what your rights are. We keep it plain and readable on purpose.

1. Data We Collect

We collect only what we need to run the Service. Here is a breakdown:

Data Type What It Is How We Get It
Name and Email Your display name and email address Provided by Whop when you sign in via OAuth
Whop User ID A unique identifier from Whop Automatically assigned at login
Subscription Status Whether you have an active plan and which tier Fetched from Whop's API at login
Quote Data Quotes you generate, including job details, client info you enter, and line items Entered by you while using the platform
Stripe Account ID Your connected Stripe Express account identifier Provided by Stripe when you connect your account
Zelle Handle Your Zelle phone number or email (optional) Entered by you in Settings
Usage Data Pages visited, features used, general platform activity Collected automatically via server logs and sessions

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect to:

  • Authenticate you and verify your subscription status through Whop
  • Deliver the QuoteBoss platform features (quote generation, PDF export, shareable links)
  • Enable payment collection for your clients via Stripe Connect
  • Store your quotes so they remain accessible via shared links
  • Improve the platform based on how it is used
  • Respond to support requests you send us

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or selling to third parties.

3. Payment Data

We do not store payment card numbers, bank account numbers, or any sensitive financial data.

All client payments are processed by Stripe. When a client pays through a QuoteBoss quote link, they enter their card details directly on Stripe's hosted Checkout page. We never see or store that card information.

What we do store:

  • Your Stripe Connect account ID (e.g., acct_xxxx), so we can route payments to you
  • Your fee preference (whether you pass the platform fee to clients or absorb it yourself)

For questions about how Stripe handles payment data, see Stripe's Privacy Policy.

4. Who We Share Data With

We share data only with the third-party services required to run QuoteBoss. We do not sell your data.

Service Purpose What They Receive
Whop Authentication and subscription management OAuth tokens, user ID, membership verification
Stripe Payment processing for client payments Connected account ID, payment amounts, quote metadata
Render Cloud hosting and infrastructure All application data (stored and processed on their servers)

Each of these providers has their own privacy policy and data handling practices. We recommend reviewing them directly.

5. Cookies and Sessions

QuoteBoss uses a server-side session (via Flask) to keep you logged in after you authenticate with Whop. A session cookie is stored in your browser for this purpose. It contains a session identifier, not your personal data directly.

We do not use third-party advertising cookies or tracking pixels. Session cookies are deleted when you log out or when they expire.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@quoteboss.io. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe, typically within 10 business days.

Note: Deleting your account will remove your profile and settings. Quote data tied to active shareable links may be retained briefly to avoid breaking links in active use.

7. Children's Privacy

QuoteBoss is a professional tool intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under the age of 13. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us and we will remove the data promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the platform evolves. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of the Service after changes take effect means you accept the updated policy.

We will not make changes that materially reduce your rights without making a reasonable effort to notify you first.
