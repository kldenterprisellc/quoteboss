Everything you need to know about QuoteBoss.

Using QuoteBoss

Pro Features
Yes, on the Pro plan. When building your quote, enter your own low and high estimates in the custom rate fields. These override the national averages and your prices are used for the quote and PDF.
Logo upload is a Pro feature currently in development. It will appear in your Settings once live. When added, your logo will replace the QuoteBoss branding in every PDF you generate.
Collecting Payments
Go to Settings and connect your Stripe account. Once connected, every quote link you send will show "Pay 50% Deposit" and "Pay in Full" buttons. Your client pays by card, money goes directly to your bank account.
There is a 1% processing fee in addition to Stripe's standard card fees (2.9% + 30 cents). By default this is added to the client's total so you receive your full quoted amount. Pro users can toggle this to absorb the fee themselves in Settings.
Yes. Add your Zelle email or phone number in Settings. It will appear on your quote links so clients can send payment directly to your Zelle. No processing fees for Zelle payments.
Stripe typically deposits funds to your bank account within 2 business days of a payment. Your payout schedule is set in your Stripe account settings.
Account and Billing
Log into your Whop account at whop.com, find QuoteBoss under your memberships, and cancel from there. No cancellation fees. You keep access until the end of your billing period.
Go to whop.com/web-growth-systems and purchase the Pro plan. Once your Whop membership is updated, sign out of QuoteBoss and sign back in - your Pro features will be unlocked automatically.
Reach out through your Whop account or email support at the address on your Whop receipt. We typically respond within a few hours.
